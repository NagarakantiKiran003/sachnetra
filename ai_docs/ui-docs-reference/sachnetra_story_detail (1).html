Floods in Assam affect 40,000 people across 3 districts

What happened

Heavy rainfall since Tuesday caused the Brahmaputra river to overflow in Barpeta, Morigaon, and Nagaon districts. Around 40,000 people have been displaced. NDRF teams are on the ground. NH-15 near Guwahati remains blocked.

What this means

If you are travelling to or from Assam, check NH-15 status before departing. Rain is forecast to continue through the weekend. Relief camps are open in all three districts.
